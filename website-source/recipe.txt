<p>
<b>Ingredients:</b>
- 1 cup cooked chicken 
- 16 oz can diced tomatoes
- 6 oz tomato paste 
- 1/2 cup chicken or beef broth 
- 1/2 cup black olives 
- 16 oz can black beans
- 12 corn tortillas
- vegetable oil 
- 1 1/2 cups shredded monterrey jack
- 1 cup sour cream 

- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder 
- 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin 
- 1 tsp chili powder 

<b>Directions: </b>
1. Preheat oven to 350 F 
2. Into a pot add tomatoes with juices, tomato paste, broth, garlic, and cumin. Crush tomatoes with back of spoon and cook until reduced, 10-12 min.
3. With two forks, shred chicken. Toss shredded chicken, beans, and olives in half of sauce, reserving half to pour over later.
4. In a casserole, spread a thin layer of sauce on bottom. Fill each tortilla with chicken mixture and a sprinkle of shredded cheese, then roll, and arrange in casserole.
5. Pour remaining sauce on top of tortillas. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
6. Bake for 12-18 minutes, until cheese is melted and crispy.

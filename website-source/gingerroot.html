Fancy Cooking

Ginger Root

Author: Brandon

Back in the day, I used to work as a line cook in a super popular local spot. I won't name-drop the place, it's probably too cool for you anyway. We used to have this amazing dish that used ginger root. I'd probably have a Michelin star by now, but they fired me, can you believe it? They said I just couldn't handle all the orders coming in, all the time. But c'mon! They'd stack up like crazy and I couldn't keep up. Anyway, I hate those guys now. Heck, I'll even give you some insider intel... if you run nc infosecucalgary.ca 9002 you can get their super secret ginger recipe that they don't want me to share.
